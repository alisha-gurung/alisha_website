The Journey

From the Peaks of Kathmandu
to the Coast of Lorne

My hospitality odyssey began amidst the vibrant heritage of Kathmandu, where the ancient tradition of welcoming guests as gods instilled in me a profound respect for service. It was there, in the heart of the Himalayas, that I learned the delicate balance between operational rigor and genuine human connection.

Navigating through diverse international markets, I honed my expertise in transforming properties into landmarks of luxury. Today, bringing this global perspective to the serene shores of Lorne, I focus on crafting bespoke experiences that resonate with quiet luxury, blending local charm with world-class standards.
